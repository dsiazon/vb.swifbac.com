About Us

Our organization, Southwest Illinois Filipino Basketball Club (SWIFBAC), is one of the perennial Filipino sports organization in the southwest Chicago suburbs.

We are a non-profit organization which aims to provide a friendly, competitive and family oriented atmosphere while promoting camaraderie and sportsmanship within the Filipino community.
